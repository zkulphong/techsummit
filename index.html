The KU Tech Summit is the University of Kansas's flagship tech conference that brings students, engineers, researchers, alumni, startups, and venture capitalists together to promote entrepreneurship in the Midwest. This year's conference will showcase six tech leaders who will present on the idea of 'The Day After Tomorrow' and what technology we can expect to see in our lifetimes. Additionally, there will be networking sessions and an expo to showcase startups and university research.
